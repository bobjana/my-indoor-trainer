Connecting Your Trainer

To connect your smart trainer or heart rate monitor:

  1. Turn it on: Make sure your device is powered on and not connected to another app (like Zwift or Wahoo).
  2. Enable Bluetooth: Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your computer or phone.
  3. Grant Permission: When you click "Connect", your browser will ask for permission to use Bluetooth. You must click "Allow".
  4. Select Device: A list of nearby devices will appear. Select your trainer from the list to connect.

Troubleshooting: If you don't see your device, try turning it off and on again, or moving closer to your computer.

Developer / Stub Mode

If you don't have a real trainer nearby, you can use the built-in stub server:

  1. Open a terminal in the /stub-server directory.
  2. Run npm install then npm start.
  3. Click the "Use Stub Trainer" button on the connect screen.
